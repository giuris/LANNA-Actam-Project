TUTORIAL

The keyboard has different functionalities, first of all you can play in two different ways:
• use your mouse!
• use your pc keyboard!
If you pay attention, in the lower left corner there are two buttons, NOTE and FREQ.
• If you press NOTE you’ll be able to view the name of the note you are playing and its associated color.
• If you press FREQ you can see the swing of the pure tone related to the note you are just playing.

EXAMPLE

A triad is a chord with three notes that can be set as thirds because their pitches work together.
If you want an example of Major Triad, press the corresponding button and choose a note and play it. The keyboard allows you to listen the third and the fifth notes associated to the one you’re playing.
• Press Major Triad and, after, press the note D. The chord played is called Dmajor.
• Press Minor Triad and, after, press the note D. The chord played is called Dmajor.
• Press Diminished Triad and, after, press the note D. The chord played is called Dmajor.
• Press Augmented Triad and, after, press the note D. The chord played is called Dmajor.
Now, try to play each one by yourself.
